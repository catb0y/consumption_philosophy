Is this really how you want to shop?

Think about it.

Mindless shopping is an everyday thing. We buy things we maybe need that most likely will just be used a couple of times. We buy cheap clothing, toys, furniture, household items that we throw away one or two years later, or even days.

You can shop differently.

You can shop adopting what we call consumption philosophy: no trash, good, durable stuff only.

The consumption philosophy checklist

  1. Do I need this?
  2. Like, really?
  3. Is it of good quality?
  4. Will I need this a year from now?
  5. Have I recently bought a very similar thing? *does not apply to collectors*
