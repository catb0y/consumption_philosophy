Is this really how you want to shop?

Think about it.

Mindless shopping is an everyday thing. We buy things we maybe need that most likely will just be used a couple of times. We buy cheap clothing that we throw away the following years.

We are not telling you not to shop.

We are telling you to shop with some consumption philosophy.

The consumption philosphy checklist

  1. Do I need this?
  2. Like, really?
  3. Will I need this a year from now?
  4. Have I recently bought a very similar thing? *does not apply to collectors*
